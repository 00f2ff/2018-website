console.log
February 2018

console.log inserts environmental consciousness and puns into logging for web development. It's inspired by an excellent, Leptodactylidaeic approach to logging pioneered by Tim Holman.

With console.log, you can now log messages to a browser console that look more like logs than they did before:

> console.log("I am a log") ( I am a log (0)

For more features and bad jokes, check out console.log on GitHub.
